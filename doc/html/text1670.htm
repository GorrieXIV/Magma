Creation








In the current implementation only cusp forms are supported.



BianchiCuspForms(F, N) : FldNum, RngOrdIdl -> ModFrmBianchi



    VorData: Rec                        Default: 



This creates the cuspidal subspace of the space of Bianchi modular forms 
over the field F (an imaginary quadratic field) on Γ0(N) with weight 2.
The level N should be an ideal in the maximal order of F.


The optional argument VorData may be set equal to VoronoiData(M) where
M is a previously computed space of forms over the same field.  This avoids
repeating the time-consuming precomputations that depend only on the field.
