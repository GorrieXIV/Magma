Introduction








A structure constant algebra A of dimension n over a ring R
can be defined in Magma by giving the n3 structure constants
aijk ∈R ( 1 ≤i, j, k ≤n) such that, if (e1, e2, ..., en)
is the basis of A,  ei * ej = ∑k = 1n aijk * ek.
Structure constant algebras may be defined over any
unital ring R. However, many operations require that R be
a Euclidean domain or even a field.
