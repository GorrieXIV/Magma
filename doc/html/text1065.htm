Universal Enveloping Algebras  and Quantum Groups










Given a semisimple Lie algebra over a field of characteristic zero,
we can construct an integral basis for its universal enveloping algebra.
Functionality for computing in these algebras is described in 
Section Universal Enveloping Algebras. Moreover, we provide functionality for computing
in their quantised versions, which are called quantum groups.
This is described in Chapter QUANTUM GROUPS.
