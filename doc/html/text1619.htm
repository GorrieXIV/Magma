Dimensions of Spaces





BrandtModuleDimension(D, N) : RngIntElt, RngIntElt -> RngIntElt



The dimension of the Brandt module of level (D, N), computed by means 
of standard formulas.





Example ModBrdt_ModBrdt:Dimension (H140E6)

In the following example we demonstrate the computation of the 
dimensions of the Brandt modules for the sequence of Eichler orders 
of index 3i in a maximal order in the quaternion algebra of 
discriminant 2.3 .7. 





> D := 2*5*7;
> for i in [0..10] do
>     BrandtModuleDimension(D,3^i);
> end for;
