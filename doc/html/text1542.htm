Projection Maps








The database contains information allowing the reconstruction of the standard projection maps between
the models of the level N and level M curves for any M dividing N.



ProjectionMap(CN,N,CM,M) : Crv, RngIntElt, Crv, RngIntElt -> MapSch



The curves CN and CM should be base changes to the same characteristic zero field K of the small modular database
curves of levels N and M with M | N.


Returns the natural projection map CN -> CM that corresponds
to z |-> z in terms of the upper half-plane quotient models and to (E, C) |-> (E, (N/M)C) in the moduli
space interpretation where non-cuspidal points correspond to isomorphism classes of an elliptic curves E
with a cyclic subgroup C of order N.



ProjectionMap(CN,N,CM,M,r) : Crv, RngIntElt, Crv, RngIntElt, RngIntElt -> MapSch



The curves CN and CM should be base changes to the same characteristic zero field K of the small modular database
curves of levels N and M with M | N and r should be a positive integer divisor of N/M.


Returns the projection map CN -> CM that corresponds
to z |-> rz in terms of the upper half-plane quotient models and to 
(E, C) |-> (E/(N/r)C, (N/(Mr))C/(N/r)C)
in the moduli space interpretation where non-cuspidal points correspond
to isomorphism classes of an elliptic curves E with a cyclic subgroup C of order N.
