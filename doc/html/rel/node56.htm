Clifford Algebras


New Features:

  • Improvements have been made to the generator assignment code for Clifford algebras and the assignment of print names via AssignNames. If Q is an n x n quadratic form, the construction
    C<x1 , x2 , ..., xn > := CliffordAlgebra(Q)
    creates a Clifford algebra of rank n and assigns the identifiers x1 , x2 , ..., xn to its generators, namely the basis elements of the underlying quadratic space of C.
