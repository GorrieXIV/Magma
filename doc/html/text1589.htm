Modular Symbols





ModularSymbols(M) : ModFrm -> SeqEnum



The sequence of characteristic 0 spaces of modular symbols 
with given sign associated to the space of modular forms M, when this makes sense.



ModularSymbols(M, sign) : ModFrm, RngIntElt -> ModSym



The sequence of characteristic 0 spaces of modular symbols 
with given sign associated to the space of modular forms M, when this makes sense.





Example ModFrm_ModularSymbols (H138E22)






> M := ModularForms(Gamma0(389),2);
> ModularSymbols(M,+1);
[
    Full Modular symbols space of level 389, weight 2, and dimension 
    33
]
> ModularSymbols(M,-1);
[
    Full Modular symbols space of level 389, weight 2, and dimension 
    32
]
