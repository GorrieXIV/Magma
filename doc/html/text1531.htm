Parametrized Structures






The modular curves X0(N) parametrize elliptic curves together with the 
structure of a cyclic isogeny, or equivalently, a cyclic subgroup scheme 
of the N-torsion of the elliptic curve.  
over the modular curve, singularities of the chosen surface may obstruct
the construction of the corresponding isogeny.  



Isogeny(E,P) : CrvEll, Pt -> MapCrvEll



Given an elliptic curve E and a point P on some X0(N) 
corresponding to a cyclic level structure on E, the function
returns an isogeny f: E -> F corresponding to P.  
The isogeny is defined by the formulae of Velu in such a way
that the pull-back of the invariant differential of F is the 
invariant differential on E.



SubgroupScheme(E,P) : CrvEll, Pt -> CrvEllSubgroup



Given an elliptic curve E and a point P on some X0(N) 
corresponding to a cyclic level structure on E, the function 
returns the subgroup scheme of E parameterized by X0(N).
