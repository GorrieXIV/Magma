Highest Weight Representations










The representations of Lie algebras and connected reductive Lie groups are 
classified by highest weights.  
In addition to being able to construct these
representations [dG01], we can compute the combinatorics of their weights.  
This includes all the functionality of the LiE system
[vLCL92].
This is described in Chapter REPRESENTATIONS OF LIE GROUPS  AND ALGEBRAS.
