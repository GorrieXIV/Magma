Cohomology





Dual(G) : GrpAb -> GrpAb, Map



Computes the dual group G *  of G and a map M from 
G x G *  to Z/mZ for m the exponent of G that allows G *  to
act on G.
The group G must be finite.



H2_G_QmodZ(G) : GrpAb -> GrpAb, Map



Computes H := H2(G, Q/Z) and a map f : H to (G x G to Z/mZ)
that will give the cocycles as maps from G x G to Z/mZ,
m := #G.



Res_H2_G_QmodZ(U, H2) : GrpAb, GrpAb -> GrpAb, Map



For a subgroup U of G and H2 = H2(G, Q/Z)
computes H2(U, Q/Z) in a compatible way together with the
restriction map into H2.


The abelian group H2 must be the result of H2_G_QmodZ as this function relies on the
attributes stored in there.
