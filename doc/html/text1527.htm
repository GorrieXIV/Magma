Introduction






Modular curves in Magma are a special type CrvMod of plane 
curve.  A modular curve X is defined in terms of standard affine 
modular polynomials which are stored in precomputed databases.  
Those modular curves presently available are defined by bivariate 
polynomials relating the j-invariant and one of several standard 
functions on X0(N).  These give singular models for X0(N) 
designed for computing isogenies of elliptic curves.
